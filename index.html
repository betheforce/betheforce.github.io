Learn Android in 4 Weeks.

Sign up for this course and you will learn :

  • How to Build and Run Android apps - using tools such as Eclipse, DDMS.
  • How to make Layouts and UI - using Drawables, Layouts etc.
  • Understand Android paradigms like - Activity, Threads, AsyncTasks, Notifications, Services etc.
  • Create fully functional Android apps from scratch during the course.
  • How to Publish your apps on Google and Amazon App stores.

How it works:

  • This is a 4 Week course with live video sessions every weekend (Sat & Sun).
  • Sessions are conducted by #1 Faculty of Android. Having worked at Adobe and VC-funded startups.
  • You can ask questions and get answers right there in these sessions.
  • Each Session is of 1 hour duration (Total 8 Sessions)
  • Small batch size - max 5 students in a batch.
