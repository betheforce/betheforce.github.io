About in4WeeksAndroid

in4WeeksAndroid was started when I found that many software engineers around me were great in what they were already doing, but were unable to learn new technologies. The main reason they attributed this to was lack of time - They were already busy in their day-to-day work. Add to this the plethora of unstructured content available online and non availability of instant help when stuck on a concept. in4WeeksAndroid makes learning Android super easy. In 4 weeks, we cover the most important paradigms of Android development. We go through each and every step - from installation of required tools, to understanding advanced concepts. I am there to answer all your queries instantly. The instructor, Adam King, is a renowned Android expert. He has taught several students in the past. He had worked at Google for most of his career as a senior computer scientist and filed multiple patents. Recently he was part of a well funded startup and handled all Android engineering work.
